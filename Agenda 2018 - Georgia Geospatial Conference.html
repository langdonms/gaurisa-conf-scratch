Monday, October 1, 2018: Workshops & Social

Monday is a day of specialty workshops,registration, and vendor setup. Lunch is provided in Athena E: 12:00 – 1:00 PM.

Don’t miss the Vendor Social & Game Night from 5:30 – 8:00 PM!

  • GIS Manager & Executive/CIO Summit – FREE
  • Cohosted by ESRI.  Contact events@gaurisa.org for details.
  • ESRI presentation: The Underutilization of GIS and How To Cure It or GIS for Leaders.. OR… The Seven
  • Elements of a Successful Enterprise GIS
  • ArcGIS tech update concentrating on the latest version of Enterprise with demos like Waze-ArcGIS
  • integration with GeoAnalytics and GeoEvent
  • Esri + Partner + User Success Story : A local user with a success story Cobb County.
  • Esri + Partner + User Success Story: TBD
  • Panel Discussion: CIO/elected official/Director.
  • “Lean Coffee discussions sessions”
  • ArcGIS Pro
8:00 am – 5:00 pm ATHENA B

ArcGIS PRO makes use of the modern ribbon interface and 64-bit processing to increase the speed and efficiency of using GIS. It allows users to create amazing maps in both 2D and 3D quickly and easily.  Tripp Corbin:  EGIS Associates 

  • ArcGIS Online
8:00 am – 12:00 pm ATHENA C

Whether you have an ArcGIS Online public account or you’re a member of an ArcGIS Online Organization with a curated collection of mapping capabilities and content at your fingertips, the right course can help you be more productive, faster.  Joshua Knight : EGIS Associates

  • Addressing
1:00 pm – 4:00 pm ATHENA C

The set of rules for assigning addresses in a given place is called an Address Reference System (ARS). Documenting the ARS for any given location is an essential part of a sound addressing program. Lessons learned from places creating new rules provide a template for documenting your ARS.5.
